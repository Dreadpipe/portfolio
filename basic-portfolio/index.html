About Me

Well met! I'm an aspiring developer and current full-time low-voltage contractor. I've spent a life immersed in technology and exploring creative facets, but none have yet to bear fruit. After enduring a few winters in Chicago- coring concrete and digging trenches- I knew I wasn't reaching my potential. Welcome to the first stage of my journey as coding student at Northwestern University!

I'm a lover of music and art, but I'm an analyst-by-nature, and I intend to pair my love of creating with my penchant for tech. Watch this space!
